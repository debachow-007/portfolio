My Hobbies

Hobbies reflect my well-rounded personality and add a creative dimension to my technical expertise. They remind me of the importance of maintaining a balanced and fulfilling life, which, in turn, enriches my approach to problem-solving and innovation in my field of work. Here are some of my favorite hobbies:

Reading
I have a deep passion for literature and am an avid reader. From classic novels to the latest in tech-related publications, I find solace and inspiration in the written word. Reading not only enhances my knowledge but also fuels my creativity.
Writing
Writing is my creative outlet, allowing me to express my thoughts and ideas. Whether it's crafting fictional tales, poetry, or novels, writing offers a unique avenue for self-expression. My first novel titled "The Room of Death", stands as testament to my dedictaion to storytelling and creativity.
Trivia
I'm also an enthusiastic trivia enthusiast. My love for absorbing and sharing interesting facts and knowledge extends to a wide range of topics, making me a dedicated participant in trivia games and quizzes.
Music
Music is a constant companion in my life. It provides a balance to my technical pursuits and offers moments of relaxation and inspiration. Exploring various genres and melodies is a way for me to recharge and find motivation. One can find me absorbed into Jagjit Singh's ghazals when I'm not working.
